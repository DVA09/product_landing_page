Learn about kvass recipe

Kvass is a fermented cereal-based low alcoholic beverage with a slightly cloudy appearance, light-brown colour and sweet-sour taste. It may be flavoured with berries, fruits, herbs or honey.

We offer our customers three different types of kvass under the trademark "Kvass". Starting from fermented kvass with the taste of baked apple, fermented kvass from traditional ingredients with a classic taste, and ending with fermented kvass with dark rye malt extract.

Who are we?

Kvass is a private company that, over its short history, has gradually evolved from a small factory into the largest enterprise producing kvass.

Thanks to a partnership with a large international holding company, the company received a stream of investments that contributed to the renewal of production lines and the optimization of processes, which allowed it to increase production volumes and market share.

Today our products are supplied to 20 countries on three continents. The company annually exports over 20 million liters of kvass.
